About

I work as a software engineer on a Python platform that handles the scheduling, content selection, and ESP campaign execution for around 285 brand domains and accounts. The codebase is north of 75,000 lines with over a thousand pytest cases under it, and I am the only person who works on it — the system has mostly grown up around me over the last five years. I started here as a digital marketing analyst and have spent most of my time since automating the parts of the job I used to do by hand, then the parts other people were doing by hand, and increasingly the parts of the system that ought to run faster than they do.

Before software, I was a CAD and 3D printing engineer designing parametric models in Fusion 360 for rapid prototyping and small-scale manufacturing. Somewhere in between I went back to school for an MS in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins.

Outside of work I have been a U.S. National Team rower at three World Rowing Championships. These days I am increasingly thinking about where to take this engineering background next — ideally somewhere the work compounds into something that genuinely helps people.
